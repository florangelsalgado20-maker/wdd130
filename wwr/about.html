Flower Expeditions

We are world leaders in white-water adventures. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to providing safe, thrilling, and environmentally friendly experiences on the wildest rivers on the planet.

Our History

Flower Expeditions was born from the passion of a group of friends for nature and adrenaline. We started with a single boat and the dream of sharing the beauty of our local rivers with the entire world.

Today, we operate on three continents and have been recognized for our outstanding safety practices. Our mission remains the same: to connect people with the untamable force of nature.
