HISTORY

Flower Expeditions was founded in 2026, during a time when people were rediscovering the beauty of their local surroundings. The pandemic shifted perspectives, and we saw an opportunity to create safe, exciting adventures right here in the Caracas mountains.
Adventure in the city
rafting4.jpg
What began as a small family operation has grown into Caracas' premier whitewater rafting company. We remain committed to our eco-friendly roots, practicing sustainable tourism and educating our guests about preserving Venezuela's beautiful natural waterways.
