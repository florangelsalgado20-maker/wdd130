About FLOWER EXPEDITIONS

"The first expeditions agency in Venezuela's Capital providing fun for the whole family including kids and pets. Prepare for unforgettable adventures connecting with nature!"

History

Founded in 2026 by Florangel Salgado, FLOWER EXPEDITIONS began with a simple idea: to help people, especially Generation Z, disconnect from their phones and reconnect with nature. What started as a small family operation has grown into Venezuela's premier rafting company, offering safe and exciting adventures for all ages.

Since our founding, we've introduced thousands of visitors to the thrill of whitewater rafting while maintaining our commitment to environmental stewardship and safety. Our expert guides are trained to provide memorable experiences that create lasting bonds with nature.
